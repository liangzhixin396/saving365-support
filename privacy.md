# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

_Last updated: 2026-04-23_

**存钱拼图** ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This page explains what information the app handles.

**存钱拼图**（下称"我们"、"本应用"）尊重并保护你的隐私。本页面说明本应用会如何处理你的数据。

---

## 1. Information We Collect / 我们收集的信息

存钱拼图 **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable information to our own servers.

- **Saving records, goals, puzzle progress / 存钱记录、目标、拼图进度**: stored locally on your device, optionally synced through **Apple iCloud (CloudKit)** under your own Apple ID. 仅保存在你的本机，可选择通过你自己的 Apple iCloud 同步。
- **Friend connections / 好友关系**: established via a 6-character invite code; only nickname, avatar, and aggregate saving progress are shared through CloudKit. 通过 6 位邀请码建立，仅共享昵称、头像与总体存钱进度。
- **Photos / Camera / 相册与相机**: used only when you choose an avatar; images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to us. 仅在你设置头像时使用，图片仅保存在本地，不会上传至我方。
- **Notifications / 通知**: used only to remind you of your daily saving task. 仅用于每日存钱任务的提醒。

存钱拼图 **不会**向我方服务器收集、存储或传输任何可识别用户身份的个人信息。

---

## 2. Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

存钱拼图 uses only **Apple's iCloud / CloudKit** for optional data sync. We do **not** integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

存钱拼图 仅使用 **Apple 官方的 iCloud / CloudKit** 进行可选的数据同步，**不**接入任何统计 SDK、广告 SDK 或第三方追踪服务。

---

## 3. Data Retention & Deletion / 数据保留与删除

- Local data can be cleared at any time via **Settings → Reset** inside the app.
- iCloud data can be removed by signing in at iCloud.com → Settings → Manage → 存钱拼图 → Delete Data, or by uninstalling the app on all devices.

本地数据可随时通过 App 内「设置 → 重置」清除；iCloud 数据可登录 iCloud.com → 设置 → 管理 → 存钱拼图 → 删除数据，或在所有设备上卸载本应用以清除。

---

## 4. Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

存钱拼图 is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

存钱拼图 适用于所有年龄层，我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的任何信息。

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策，最新版本将始终发布在本页面。
