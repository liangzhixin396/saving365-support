## ❓ FAQ / 常见问题

### 1. How do I start a saving plan? / 如何开始一个存钱计划？
Open the app → tap **"Start"** on the home screen → set your goal amount and start date → confirm.
打开 App → 首页点击「开始」→ 设置目标金额与开始日期 → 确认即可。

### 2. Is my data uploaded to a server? / 我的数据会上传到服务器吗？
All your saving records are stored **locally on your device** and synced across your own devices via **iCloud (CloudKit)**. We do not collect or upload your personal data to any third-party server.
所有存钱记录仅保存在**你自己的设备**上，并通过 **iCloud (CloudKit)** 在你自己的设备之间同步。我们不会将你的个人数据上传到任何第三方服务器。

### 3. How does the friends feature work? / 好友功能是如何工作的？
Friends are linked through a 6-character invite code. Only your nickname, avatar, and overall saving progress are shared — no sensitive information.
好友通过 6 位邀请码建立连接，仅共享昵称、头像和总体存钱进度，不涉及任何敏感信息。

### 4. Why can't I see my Widget / Apple Watch data? / 为什么看不到小组件或手表上的数据？
- Make sure both the app and the widget/watch app are on the **same Apple ID**.
- Ensure iCloud is enabled on both devices.
- Open the main app at least once after installing the widget or watch app.

请确认：主 App 与小组件 / Watch App 使用**同一个 Apple ID**；两端都已开启 iCloud；安装后至少启动过一次主 App。

### 5. How do I delete my data? / 如何删除我的数据？
Go to **Settings → Reset** inside the app to clear all local data. To remove iCloud data, sign into iCloud.com → Settings → Manage → 存钱拼图 → Delete Data.
在 App 内进入「设置 → 重置」即可清除所有本地数据。如需删除 iCloud 数据，请登录 iCloud.com → 设置 → 管理 → 存钱拼图 → 删除数据。

### 6. Is 存钱拼图 free? / 这款 App 免费吗？
Yes, 存钱拼图 is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.
是的，存钱拼图 完全免费，无广告、无内购。
